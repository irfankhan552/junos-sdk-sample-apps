Welcome to the SDK Your Net Corporation (SYNC) Policy Manager Captive Portal Example

 

Using this site you may authorize or repudiate your own access. Until you are authorized, all of your traffic will come back here to the captive portal. Once you have authorized yourself, you remain authroized so long as the captive portal daemon is running. You will also only be able to return to this site manually, so you may want to bookmark or remember it.

When you have been authorized, access will be granted to your IP address, and you will be able to connect to locations outside of the captive portal. You will also be able to repudiate your access.

 

Validate actions are: Check status | Authorize | Repudiate

Note: Choosing to repudiate access when you do not have it, or to authorize access when you do have it, does nothing.
